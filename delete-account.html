Account Deletion

Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Bricole account and all associated data at any time, either directly from the app or by contacting us via email.

Option 1 — Delete from the app (recommended)

  1. Open Bricole on your device.
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  4. Type DELETE in the confirmation field to confirm.
  5. Tap Delete Permanently. Your account will be removed immediately.

Option 2 — Request by email

If you no longer have access to the app, send a deletion request to tozankayra4@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

⚠ Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. Your shots, tables, progress stats, and Pro subscription history will be erased. Active subscriptions are not automatically cancelled — please cancel through the App Store or Google Play before deleting.
